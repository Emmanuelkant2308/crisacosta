BIO

I am a self-taught developer who is currently obsessing with creating pages using HTML5, CSS3 w/ SASS and vanilla JS: ES5 & ES6.

I got into software development because I wanted to create an app and so I did my research and discovered the amazing world of programming and fell in love with it - yep, cliche. Anyways, I have been using online platforms such as Freecodecamp.org, w3schools, MDN, Youtube and Dev.to as my source to further improve myself as developer.

In the world of Software Development I am but a seed that is looking for place to sprout my roots on and grow to become a good developer, in return I am ready to be a sponge and absorb every technology that you want me learn and become a valuable asset to your team and company's growth.
